About The Website

The Complete Survival Guide to Thriving in Tech-Related College Programs, aims to serve as a helpful guide for students studying in tech-related fields through insightful blogs of personal experiences enriched with wisdom and lessons from the authors. The topics, ranging from interpretational relationships to career anxiety, are situations that students commonly face in their college life.

Meet the Authors

Our writers are fellow tech students who provide genuine stories, insights, and guidance drawn from their personal experiences. Every article is crafted to assist others in learning, developing, and thriving because we believe that the most effective advice comes from shared experience.
