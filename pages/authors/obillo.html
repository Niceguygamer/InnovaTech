Mon Andrei E. Obillo
BS Computer Science

I am a BS Computer Science student in my second year, and I try to approach both school and life with consistency, creativity, and attention to detail. I have learned that keeping up is never just about working harder, but about staying adaptable and finding better ways to move through everything on my plate.

A big part of that growth has come from balancing academic work with extracurricular responsibilities, especially as a member of the University Chorale at the University of the East - Manila. I used to be more easy-going, but over time, the pressure of constant deadlines and limited time taught me how to be more disciplined, reliable, and intentional with my effort.

Outside of school, I value time with the people I love and the responsibilities I have committed to. I am still learning and building my path forward, but I hope to be known one day for doing meaningful work, growing through the challenges I meet, and eventually finding success in both my career and the life I create for myself.
