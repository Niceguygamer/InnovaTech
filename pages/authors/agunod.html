BS Computer Science

I am a BS Computer Science student who is passionate about building innovative software solutions. When I am not coding, you will probably find me playing League of Legends — a hobby that surprisingly fuels my creativity and sharpens my problem solving more than I expected.

My goal is to work as a software or cloud engineer, building scalable and effective systems that bring real ideas to life through code. I am driven by the challenge of making things work not just functionally, but efficiently and at scale.

On top of my technical goals, I also want to work abroad — specifically in Switzerland — where I can grow professionally while experiencing a completely different culture and environment. I am dedicated to continuously learning and pushing myself further in the tech industry.

For InnovaTech, I wrote about study habits. Not because I have it all figured out, but because surviving a tech program teaches you a lot about what actually works and what does not — and those lessons are worth sharing.
