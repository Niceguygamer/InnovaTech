Euwin Johnmiel C. Leongsun
BS Computer Science

I am a BS Computer Science student who has been building things since senior high. It started with wanting to make something real, something I could actually use and share with other people, not just assignments that get submitted and forgotten.

That drive led me to Prior, a platform I built and have been developing since. It is a full-stack project that covers a social network, AI services, media tools, real-time chat, and more. Building Prior taught me more about software than any single course, mostly because it forced me to figure things out, break things, and fix them.

For InnovaTech, I wrote about two things I know firsthand: competence and burnout. Both shaped how I work and how I think about surviving a tech program without losing the part of you that actually enjoys building things.
