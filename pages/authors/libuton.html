BS Computer Science

I am a BS Computer Science student who discovered a liking for computing in seventh grade. What began as basic curiosity quickly became a true fascination, particularly in HTML, where I enjoyed building and designing my own web pages.

I spent a lot of my time experimenting, making random websites and applying what I learnt on platforms like Roblox. These experiences not only helped me enhance my knowledge of technology, but also linked me with others who shared my interests. As a result, I became a member of a creative and supportive group where I worked on projects, created games, and learned by creating.

These early experiences influenced my approach to technology now. I remain curious and open to new experiences, always looking for ways to better my knowledge and expand on what I currently know. Continuous practice, and a willingness to learn at my own speed are what drive my technological advancement.
