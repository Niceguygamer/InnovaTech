Charlize Jane A. Alab-ab
BS Computer Science

As a computer science student, I have an unwavering love of computers and technology. I enjoy programming, building software programs and creating new technologies; however, I do not particularly enjoy doing math. Nonetheless, I am still very enthusiastic about learning and exploring new concepts in the area of computer science.

In my role as contributor and developer for Innovatech's website, my goal is to share knowledge and ideas that inspire others. One of my contributions is a research project involving 10 subtopics that are related to the concept of study app use in education. These subtopics demonstrate how study apps can be a vital asset or 'weapon' in terms of achieving success within an academic setting. My goal for this contribution is to guide students through the use of technology as a means to develop more productive learning strategies and ultimately perform better academically.

As I continue my journey, I strive to grow my skills, contribute meaningful content, and make technology more accessible and helpful for others.
